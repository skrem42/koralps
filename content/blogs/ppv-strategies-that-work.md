# PPV Strategies That Actually Work in 2025

If you're not maximizing your PPV (pay-per-view) revenue, you're leaving serious money on the table.

Here's the truth: **PPV should be 40-60% of your total OnlyFans income**.

If it's not, keep reading.

## Why Most Creators Fail at PPV

The #1 mistake? Treating all subscribers the same.

Not everyone on your page has the same:
- Budget
- Interests
- Relationship with you

Yet most creators send the same mass PPV to everyone. That's leaving money on the table.

## The Segmentation Strategy

Top earners segment their audience into tiers:

### Tier 1: Whales (Top 5% spenders)
- Personalized messages
- Exclusive content first
- Higher price points ($50-100+)
- VIP treatment

### Tier 2: Regular Spenders (20%)
- Premium PPV offerings
- Bundle deals
- $20-50 price range

### Tier 3: Casual Fans (75%)
- Mass PPV at lower prices
- Teasers to upgrade
- $10-20 range

## The Perfect PPV Message Formula

1. **Personal greeting** - Use their name
2. **Build anticipation** - Describe what they're getting
3. **Create urgency** - Limited time or exclusive
4. **Clear CTA** - Tell them to unlock

## Timing Matters

Best times to send PPV:
- Friday evening (payday vibes)
- Sunday afternoon (relaxation time)
- After engagement (strike while hot)

Worst times:
- Monday morning
- Late night (except for night owls)

## The Follow-Up Sequence

Don't just send once and forget. Use this sequence:

1. **Initial send** - The main pitch
2. **24 hours later** - "Did you see this?"
3. **48 hours later** - Discount offer for non-buyers
4. **72 hours** - Last chance / expires soon

## Pricing Psychology

- **$9.99** feels like "under $10"
- **$19.99** is the sweet spot for most content
- **$49.99** for premium/exclusive
- **Custom prices** for whales (they'll pay more)
