## üß† Core Psychology Principles Used

Both funnels leverage these proven tactics:

### 1. **Guarantee-First Approach**
Start with the biggest promise to grab attention immediately.

### 2. **Problem Agitation**
Make the prospect feel understood before presenting solution.

### 3. **Complete System Positioning**
Not just one service, but everything needed (vs. piecemeal solutions).

### 4. **Hybrid Model Differentiation**
"Not just X, not just Y" - position as the only complete solution.

### 5. **Bottleneck Methodology**
Fix one constraint at a time (not overwhelming "fix everything").

### 6. **Coach + Done-For-You**
The powerful combination of execution + strategy.

### 7. **Progressive Commitment**
Multiple CTAs throughout, leading to final application.

### 8. **Qualification Language**
"See if you qualify" vs. "Sign up now" - increases desire.

### 9. **No-Pressure Close**
Explicitly remove fear: no credit card, no selling, just a chat.

### 10. **Guarantee Repetition**
State the guarantee 3+ times throughout the funnel.
